gift a Year of education to Rural children

In the current academic year, eVidyaloka programs helped educate 25 lac children in rural areas across 11 states of India with the support of our 4000 strong networks of volunteers. However, there are millions more across the country who are at risk of dropping out of formal education due to the closure of schools and no access to online learning.

With your generous donations, eVidyaloka will be able to deliver quality education to more rural communities in the coming year and ensure a promising future for the nation’s children.
