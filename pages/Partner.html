eVidyaloka Trust works in a strong partner ecosystem to realize the vision of empowering children of rural India through quality digital education. Over the years, eVidyaloka has collaborated successfully with several like-minded organizations to deliver our program to the last mile learners.

We believe that anyone who shares our vision is a potential partner. We invite registered trusts and societies working with children at the grassroots level to join our mission. Groups and Individuals passionate to bring positive change in the society are welcome. Private or Aided Schools can also avail the program for their students.

Our network partners are the strongest pillars of the eVidyaloka program, delivering high-scale impact in the community. Collaboration with eVidyaloka leads to sustained resources and increased visibility for the partners, thereby accelerating the transformation of the rural educational landscape. We also build capacity in our partners for a strong and sustainable relationship.

Join us today and be part of the nation-wide EdTech movement!
