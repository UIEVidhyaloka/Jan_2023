Volunteer today,

To make a difference to the children of rural India.

Why should you volunteer?

2 hours of your time.

Impact that lasts a lifetime.

(And many more)

  • You help bring quality education to rural India. You help shape the future.

  • The learning is both ways. You learn as much as the children learn from you.

  • The children learn from you beyond the curriculum

  • Your contribution brings about lasting change in a community.

  • You are part of a volunteering movement, that has members from all over the world.

Can you volunteer?

Our volunteers are from across the globe, from a diverse background of

  • Homemakers

  • Corporate Employees

  • Retired professionals

  • Teachers

  • UG & PG Students

We look for 3 factors, they should be able to dedicate two hours for classes on weekdays, they must know how to read, write, and speak in at least one Indian language, should have a laptop or Desktop with the Webcam and internet connection. At eVidyaloka, we are super proud of our volunteers, they bridge the gap created by the lack of quality teachers.
