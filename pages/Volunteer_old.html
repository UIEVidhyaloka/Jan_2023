Volunteering at eVidyaloka

These eleven years have been an amazing journey for eVidyaloka with our volunteers. We believe that each one of our volunteers is passionate about making a difference in the lives of the children. We have volunteers across the globe, coming from a diverse background of Homemakers, Corporate Employees, and Retired professionals, Teachers, UG & PG Students. We look for 3 factors, they should be able to dedicate two hours for classes on weekdays, they must know how to read, write, and speak in at least one Indian language, should have a laptop or Desktop with the Webcam and internet connection. At eVidyaloka, we are super proud of our volunteers, they bridge the gap created by the lack of quality teachers.

Benefits & Impact of Volunteering

  • Joyful teaching experience or contributing through good content

  • Being sensitized to the needs of rural children which does not happen in daily life.

  • Making a difference! A whole new purpose in life

  • Being a part of an evolved community and upgrading the lives of the underprivileged.

  • Cause to expand your system and meet new people

What are the opportunities- Program Links/Registration link

  1. Digital School Program - to ensure that no child is deprived of learning! This program aims to provide multimedia content aligned with the school curriculum in a ready-to-consume format so children from even the remotest villages in India can access quality learning content just like their urban counterparts. In this program, Volunteer-teachers virtually engage with the students and teach/ revise the topics in live sessions, if the concepts are not clearly understood by the students. A volunteer Teaches English, Science or Mathematics subjects to class 5th - 10th-grade students.

  2. Digital Classroom Program- connecting passionate individuals as volunteer teachers from across the world with the Govt elementary schools in remote and rural villages of India, leveraging technology. It is focused on the children in the age group of 10-14 (5th to 8th grade), delivering live interactive classes in the local medium, through a powerful partner ecosystem.

  3. Create study Materials- Help the students to remember the concepts easily by providing study Materials with more images/videos than texts related to the topic which helps them learn and recollect easily.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age group of the students that this program is focusing on?

We are focusing on the students of age group 10 - 14 years, i.e., Grade 5 to Grade 8

Which are the subjects that I can teach and which curriculum is followed?

We follow the state board curriculum for Math and Science and Foundational English as well.

How much time should we contribute to teaching?

You are requested to spend 2 hours a week for live sessions.

You may also need to spend some extra hours to prepare for the classes, mark the student attendance and set up assessment papers, etc.

Can we take the classes through mobile?

No. The volunteers are requested to conduct the sessions either from a laptop or from a desktop

What will be the class timing?

The classes usually happen on weekdays between 8.00 am â€“ 4.00 pm.

In case I am not available for one class, can I reschedule the same?

Yes. Can be informed to Class Assistant (physically present in the centres) for the same.

I can only speak the regional Language. Can I be a part of this program?

No. You should know how to read and write in the respective regional medium.

How do I know whether students are able to understand my classes?

Take regular feedback from the DSM (Digital School Manager) and ask students to solve the worksheets.

Is this program available in other languages as well?

The program is available in Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu & Kannada.

What will be the student count?

The Student count varies from 20 -to 50, depending on the Grade and School

Who will be the point of contact for me?

Class Assistant, DSM (Digital School Manager), Respective state coordinator from evidyaloka

Will you share the topic list before my session?

Yes.

Do I need to buy textbooks or blackboard/whiteboards to teach?

You donâ€™t need to buy the textbooks. The textbook links will be available on wikividya - You can use online versions of blackboards/whiteboards to teach the students.

How do I mark the attendance of the students and set up the assessment sheets?

During the orientation, the coordinator will take you through the portal and will show you how to mark your attendance. The sample/format of the assessment sheet also will be shared with you in advance

In case, I am unable to take one planned session, what should I do?

You can reschedule the missed class with the help of Class Assistant/Digital School Manager

Is the timing flexible? Can I choose the subject, time and days to teach?

The timings are fixed as the requirements have come from the student side. You may share your preferences. Based on your preferences and the requirements, we will assign you to a centre.

How do the classes happen?

The classes happen through Google Meet/Skype. - You are requested to create a GMeet link/Skype account and share it with the coordinator

Can I teach more than one subject or one grade?

Yes. You can definitely teach more than one subject or one grade, provided you can give us the required hours of commitment.

Will you be providing the content support or do I need to prepare myself?

The textbook, videos and worksheets will be shared with you.

However, if needed, you are welcome to create your own worksheets for your set of students

Will I get a certificate if I volunteer for this program?

You will be eligible for the certificate once you complete a certain number of sessions. - Certificates will be shared at the end of the Academic year.

What is the minimum commitment required for this program?

You are requested to give us a minimum commitment of 3-4 months.

Can I refer my family/friends for this program?

Definitely. We would love to have your family/friends join us.

Can I volunteer for any other program along with this teaching program?

Yes, you can. Please make sure that you have enough time to volunteer for the programs that you have opted for.

In case I am unable to continue this program, what should I do?

Please inform the Digital School Manager and the state coordinator in eVidyaloka team

How many classes can we cancel in a month?

As minimum as possible. We request you to avoid any last-minute cancellations. Kindly give 12 hr prior intimation to the coordinator - However, if there is an emergency, request you to inform the Class Assistant/digital school manager and the eVidyaloka team through WhatsApp/Call
