eVidyaloka is a not-for-profit organization that focuses on transforming the educational landscape of rural India. Registered under the Trusts Act, the organization creates digital classrooms for children in remote villages, with the support of local communities and Volunteer Teachers from across the globe. With standardized lesson plans and teaching aids for a consistent learning experience, the children from government schools receive e-quality education for a promising & rewarding future.

History: eVidyaloka was founded in Jan, 2011, with co-founders Satish and Venkat, colleagues at Microsoft India, with the passion to bring volunteerism and technology together, to enable quality in education for most deserving children of remote and rural villages of India. After a pilot of the idea on the field for two years, the journey was set in motion with full time employees since May, 2013.

eVidyaloka Education for All Network (eVean) Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit social enterprise, based out of Bengaluru. Its vision is to create a knowledgeable and empowered rural India through quality education. We work in partnership with local communities and the government to make a meaningful impact in the lives of these children.

eVean Foundation enables capacity building among local communities to run the digital classroom program independently. Through our technology-driven platform, we provide volunteer teachers who conduct online classes primarily in English, Mathematics, and Science, for the students of 5th-8th Std. We also extend our platform services to interested organizations/individuals who wish to implement similar projects in their areas.
