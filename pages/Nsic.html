Frequently Asked Question

The most common questions about how NSIC works and what can do for you.

Who can register as participants ?

Children from grades 6, 7, and 8 can register.

What is the eligibility ?

Interest in community service, an aptitude to solve problems, with internet connectivity and a device to operate.

Who can be an intern ?

High school students of class 11 and 12 looking to develop leadership and management skills. They would get an internship certificate from eVidyaloka after the successful completion of the program.

Who can be a mentor ?

An adult with a community development orientation and a passion to work with children for a cause.

What is the time duration of the program ?

12 - 15 weeks

Is there a seperate registrations for the mentor ?

No, the mentors would be approached by the participants. The details would be added in the registration form.

How should the final report be submitted ?

The final report could be a simple document along with videos/audios, pictures of all your efforts. Could be presented as a combination of English and regional languages.

What would be the criteria for evaluation of the contest ?

A. Orginatlity of thoughts of the children,

B. Creativity, correctness and depth of the details provided,

C. Alignment to the problem statement,

D. Participation level of the team as a whole and

E. Adhering to the timeliness of submission.

Publishing and sharing photos and videos of the team connect will add boost to the process.

What is the team composition ?

2 children + 1 mentor per team

What would be the topics to work on ?

UN Sustainable Development Goals/ UN Millennium Development Goals. The problem statement would be provided by the core team at the beginning of the program.

How will the participants of the team communicate with each other ?

Virtual Communication. Skype/Whatsapp/Google meet applications could be used.

What is the role of the mentor ?

The role of the mentor is to guide and support the team towards achieving a solution to the socio economic problem. It is important to note that the ideas and creativity should be originating from the children, while the mentor should be only directing them towards the solution.

What is the role of the intern ?

The intern would be coordinating with the mentors for the progress of the respective teams and help them bridge the gap between students and mentors , also fecilitate in the smooth conduction of the overall program.

What are the steps to the entire program ?

STEP 1. Register for the program using the respective links STEP 2. Await for the problem statements to be assigned to your team STEP 3. Start working on your solution through reading, collecting data and analysis. STEP 4. Create a final compact report, including vedios and photographs to explain your solution. STEP 5. Await further instructions from the core team for final vedio presentation details (size, time, etc)

Can we seek the references and data from the internet and related web sites ?

google and internet webistes are to be used only as references. Plagiarism will strictly not be entertained and such teams would be eliminated from the program.
